The bot also capture the video of the entire interview process for evaluation purposes

Timed Based Process

For each technical question bot assigns the time-limit within which the candidate has to record the answer else bot will automatically moves to next question

Before you start, know how it works:


Points To Remember:

  1. This is just the first step on entire interview process

  2. The automated bot will conduct the first level of interview

  3. Based on the profile you have applied for, the bot will present the option to choose from the different skills like C#, Angular, SQL or Java, Angular, Oracle etc. required for the given profile

  4. Based on your skill selection the bot will start asking questions related to that skill

  5. Once all the questions for the given skill are attempted the bot will present the option to choose next skill

  6. The above process will continue till all the sections are attempted

  7. You can attempt the required sections in any order

  8. The interview will consist of mixed of objective and descriptive type of questions

  9. For objective type of questions, bot will present the different options to choose from

  10. On successfully attempting all the required sections, bot will confirm the successful attempt of interview process and terminate the conversation

  11. Internally notifications would be send out to respective panel for review who will evaluate your answers and provide feedback to HR personnel

  12. During this entire time, HR personnel would be in touch with you and also take care of the next proceedings

  13. Before starting with the interview process, we suggest you to try out the tutorial to get familiar with the bot and different user controls
